Maven Libera Capital is now conducting a comprehensive feasibility study for the Mount Isa Critical Minerals and Rare Earth Research Centre. Building on their earlier scoping study — which confirmed market demand, and identified a pathway to operational self-sufficiency — the full feasibility study will deliver detailed engineering, refined financial modelling, governance structure, and a construction-ready business case for the centre. The centre will be located in Mount Isa which is in the North West Minerals Province.
